What are soft circuits?

Also known as e-textiles or wearable computing, soft circuits involve using textile conductive material like thread and cloth to make simple circuits to use electronic components.

Why do this?

  • Self-efficacy - Accessible entry point to learn broader concepts about electronics
  • Creativity - Hack your own clothing and environment in ways that suit you.
  • Empowerment - Wearable computing is becoming more ubiquitous. Knowlege of how these work can make you a more thoughtful consumer and critic
