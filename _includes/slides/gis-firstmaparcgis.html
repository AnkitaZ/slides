GIS Workshop: Making Your First Map

Thursday, September 3
3:00 - 4:00pm
Alderman Library, Room 421

Getting started with new software can be intimidating. This workshop introduces the skills you need to work with spatial goodness. Along the way you’ll get a taste of Earth’s most popular geographic software and a gentle introduction to map making. You’ll leave with your own cartographic masterpiece and tips for learning more in your pursuit of mappiness at UVa. Instructor: Chris Gist.
